THE YEAR OF RAT

On January 25, 2020, with the new moon of Aquarius, a new year begins in the Chinese lunar calendar. The Year of the Metal Rat. In the Chinese Horoscope the Rat is the first sign and marks the beginning of a new 12-year energy cycle. A new Chinese wheel begins, that is, the year of the rat marks the beginning of a new cycle that brings the possibility of leaving behind all the negatives of the past and activating new energies. All signs will be influenced by the powerful energy of this little animal that drives action and new beginnings. Stimulates intelligence, cunning, charm and charisma. The Metal element marks a year of optimism, a search for harmony, consolidation and radical changes.
